**Block Breaker** games have the player control a paddle at the bottom of the screen to bounce a ball upward, clearing a field of blocks arranged at the top of the screen.

This subgenre comes as an evolution of [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/11. Arcade/11.01. Ball and Paddle\|Ball and Paddle]] games, with *Breakout* ([[80 - 89 Resources/81. Companies/81.03. Atari\|Atari]], 1976) and *Arkanoid* ([[80 - 89 Resources/81. Companies/81.02. Taito\|Taito]], 1986) among the more recognizable.
