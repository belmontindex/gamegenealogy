**Arcade Snake** games require the player to collect items on a field while avoiding an ever-increasing trail left by their movement.

>[!warning] ***Under Construction!***

I don't have a lot to say about this subgenre, because not too much has changed from the time between *Blockade* ([[80 - 89 Resources/81. Companies/81.01. Sega\|Sega]], 1976) and the likes of modern *[slither.io](http://slither.com/io)*.

Oh, but the release of *Qix* ([[80 - 89 Resources/81. Companies/81.02. Taito\|Taito]], 1981) did spawn the super subgenre of Arcade Revealer Snake games, where the length of a player's trail could be turned on or off to draw complete perimeters around areas on the playing field.
