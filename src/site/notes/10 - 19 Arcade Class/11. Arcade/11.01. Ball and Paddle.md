**Ball and Paddle** games have the player move along a fixed axis to bounce an object away from entering their field.

>[!warning] ***Under Construction!***

This genre is most commonly associated with *Pong* (Atari, 1972). However, occasional arcade releases built on the formula through the 1990s, notably including *Windjammers* (Data East, 1994).
