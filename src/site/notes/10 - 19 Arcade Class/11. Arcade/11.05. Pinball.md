**Pinball** games are an [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/11. Arcade/11.00. Arcade Genre\|Arcade]] subgenre where you… play pinball. You know. Pinball.

The specific execution can vary, with games like *Alien Crush* (pictured below, [[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.12. Compile\|Compile]], 1988) taking a more fantastical approach and others like *Pinball FX* (Zen Studios, 2023) opting for the simulation of real-life machines.

...it's also a good subgenre to reskin with your favorite mascot franchise, from Sonic to Metroid.
