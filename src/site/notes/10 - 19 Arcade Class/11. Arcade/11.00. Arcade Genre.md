**Arcade** games include a variety of gameplay styles, and those included in this genre are essentially that which epitomize the [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/10. Arcade Class\|Arcade Class]]. These games emphasize replayability, with the audience intended to master the mechanics for higher scores.

Though the genre is named after the machines its games originated from, non-arcade games like *Blast Corps* ([[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.13. Rare\|Rare]], 1998) or *Katamari Damacy* ([[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.04. Namco\|Namco]], 2004) have brought the arcade experience to home consoles.
