**Brawlers** center around eliminating enemies, typically with melee attacks.

## Subgenres

### [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/13. Brawling/13.01. Side-Scrolling Brawler\|Side-Scrolling Brawler]]

**Side-Scrolling Brawlers** are the distant cousin of [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/16. Platforming/16.03. Action Platformer\|Action Platformers]]. While both feature a gravity-bound player character attempting to reach the end of the stage, Side-Scrolling Brawlers place more of an emphasis on overcoming enemy attack patterns when compared to the latter's primary focus on platforming challenges.

*Altered Beast* ([[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.01. Sega\|Sega]], 1988); *Viewtiful Joe* ([[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.07. Capcom\|Capcom]], 2003)

### [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/13. Brawling/13.02. Belt-Scrolling Brawler\|Belt-Scrolling Brawler]]

**Belt-Scrolling Brawlers** swap verticality for wide-open spaces and favor waves of enemies compared to the continuous stream often found in Side-Scrolling Brawlers. Belt-Scrolling Brawlers are well known for their combo systems, disposable weapons, and... food found in trash cans.

### [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/13. Brawling/13.03. Adventure Brawler\|Adventure Brawler]]

**Adventure Brawlers** bring the genre's core mechanics to the [[20 - 29 Adventure Class/20. Adventure Class\|Adventure Class]], often featuring heavier stories, puzzle-solving, and exploration.

<video src="https://dillonbelmont.com/02genealogy-assets/13/13.03/brawlerad.mp4" controls width="100%"></video>
*Onimusha* ([[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.07. Capcom\|Capcom]], 2001); *God of War* (Santa Monica Studio, 2005)

### [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/13. Brawling/13.04. Spectacle Brawler\|Spectacle Brawler]]

**Spectacle Brawlers** emphasize flashy character-focused combos. While still featuring many of the additions Adventure Brawlers brought to the genre, Spectacle Brawlers retain an Arcade Class backbone with fast-paced action and replayable segments intended for high score achievement.

<video src="https://dillonbelmont.com/02genealogy-assets/13/13.04/brawlersp.mp4" controls width="100%"></video>
*Bayonetta 2* ([[90 - 99 Resources/91. Companies/91.15. PlatinumGames\|PlatinumGames]], 2014)

### [[10 - 19 Arcade Class/13. Brawling/13.05. Onslaught Brawler\|Onslaught Brawler]]

**Onslaught Brawlers** task the player with defeating armies as a single character while simultaneously commanding troops.

<video src="https://dillonbelmont.com/02genealogy-assets/13/13.05/brawleros.mp4" controls width="100%"></video>
